COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Commercial Lease Agreement is entered into between ABC Corp (Landlord) and XYZ Business (Tenant).

TERM AND RENT
The lease term is for 5 years commencing January 1, 2024. Monthly rent is $5,000 payable on the first day of each month.

LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from all claims arising from Tenant's use of the premises. Landlord's liability is limited to actual damages not exceeding one month's rent.

TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice for material breach that remains uncured after notice period.

GOVERNING LAW
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of California.
